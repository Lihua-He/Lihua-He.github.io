About me

I received my B.Sc. degree in School of Computer Science and Technology in June 2017 from Yunnan Minzu University. In the same year, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc. degree in School of Computer Science and Technology from Yunnan Minzu University without entrance examination. From Sept. 2020, I started my Ph.D. degree in Computer Applied Technology at Macao Polytechnic University (MPU) under the supervision of Dr. Wuman Luo from MPU. My research interest is spatial-temporal data mining.
