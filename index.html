About me

Hi, I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Applied Technology at Macao Polytechnic University (MPU) , advised by Dr. Wuman Luo from MPU. I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology in June 2017 from Yunnan Minzu University (YMU). In the same year, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc. degree in Mathematics from YMU without entrance examination. My research interest is spatial-temporal data mining.
